# Buttonify ⏻

A simple FFmpeg-based web app to convert user videos to old school 88x31px buttons.

![Preview](/public/Preview.gif)

*^^^ See? Nothing hard at all!*

## 📋 Quickstart guide 

  1) Drag and drop your target video to the website
  2) Change the settings on top according to your needs and press <kbd>Convert</kbd>
  3) Wait until the conversion is done and now you can obtain your button by pressing <kbd>Download</kbd>
  4) Add it to your [Neocity](https://neocities.org/) or another kind of web space and share it with others this way

## 🚀 Tech stack

* **Frontend:** HTML5, TypeScript, [Svelte](https://svelte.dev/) 🔥
* **Styling:** CSS3, [Tailwind CSS](https://tailwindcss.com/), [Daisy UI](https://daisyui.com/) 🌼
